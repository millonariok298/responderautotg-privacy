Privacy Policy / Política de Privacidad

Last updated: December 2024

1. Overview

ResponderAutoTG ("the App") is an automatic response tool for Telegram messages. This policy describes how we handle your information.

2. Data We Access

The App accesses:

  • Telegram Notifications: To detect incoming messages
  • Message Content: Only for local processing and sending automatic responses
  • User Configuration: Schedules, response templates, and preferences

3. How We Use Data

ALL PROCESSING IS LOCAL:

  • Messages are processed only on your device
  • We do not send, store, or share your messages with external servers
  • Automatic responses are generated and sent directly from your device
  • Your configuration is saved only on your device

4. AI API Integration (Optional)

If you enable AI responses:

  • You can connect your own ChatGPT or Google Gemini API key
  • Messages are sent to these services ONLY if you configure it
  • These services have their own privacy policies
  • You control when and how this feature is used

5. Required Permissions

  • Notification Access: To read Telegram messages
  • Foreground Service: To keep the app active and respond automatically
  • Internet: Only if you use AI API integration (optional)

6. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We have no servers or external databases
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the application

7. Data Sharing

WE DO NOT share, sell, or transfer your personal data to third parties. The application does not collect personally identifiable user information.

8. Security

Since all processing is local:

  • Your messages never leave your device (except if you configure AI APIs)
  • There is no risk of data leaks on servers
  • Your information is protected by your device's own security

9. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through an app update.

11. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:

  • हम आपके संदेशों को बाहरी सर्वर पर नहीं भेजते, संग्रहीत या साझा नहीं करते हैं
  • स्वचालित प्रतिक्रियाएं सीधे आपके डिवाइस से उत्पन्न और भेजी जाती हैं
  • आपका कॉन्फ़िगरेशन केवल आपके डिवाइस पर सहेजा जाता है

4. AI API एकीकरण (वैकल्पिक)

  • आप अपनी खुद की ChatGPT या Google Gemini API कुंजी कनेक्ट कर सकते हैं
  • संदेश इन सेवाओं को केवल तभी भेजे जाते हैं जब आप इसे कॉन्फ़िगर करते हैं
  • इन सेवाओं की अपनी गोपनीयता नीतियां हैं
  • आप नियंत्रित करते हैं कि यह सुविधा कब और कैसे उपयोग की जाती है

5. आवश्यक अनुमतियां
